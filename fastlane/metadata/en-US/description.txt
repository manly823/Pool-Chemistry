Pool Chemistry is your personal water quality laboratory, designed to help pool owners maintain crystal clear, safe, and perfectly balanced water with scientific precision and ease.

Track every essential water parameter including pH, free chlorine, total chlorine, total alkalinity, cyanuric acid (CYA/stabilizer), calcium hardness, and water temperature. Each parameter is displayed on a beautifully designed gauge that instantly shows whether your levels are in the ideal range, approaching warning thresholds, or require immediate attention with intuitive color-coded indicators.

The built-in Dosage Calculator takes the guesswork out of pool maintenance. Simply select the parameter you want to adjust, enter your current reading and desired target, and Pool Chemistry calculates the exact amount of chemical needed for your specific pool volume. The calculator supports multiple chemical options — for example, when lowering pH you can choose between muriatic acid or dry acid — and provides clear instructions for safe application. All calculations are based on industry-standard formulas used by professional pool technicians.

Monitor your water quality over time with detailed trend charts that reveal patterns in your pool chemistry. Spot seasonal drifts, track the impact of heavy usage or weather events, and make data-driven decisions about your maintenance routine. The Water Quality Score provides an at-a-glance aggregate assessment of your pool health on a 0-100 scale.

Keep a complete chemical inventory with remaining quantities, purchase dates, and low-stock alerts so you never run out of essential supplies mid-season. Organize chemicals by category — sanitizers, pH adjusters, alkalinity balancers, stabilizers, calcium products, shock treatments, algaecides, and clarifiers.

Set your preferred testing schedule and receive timely push notification reminders. Whether you test daily, every other day, twice a week, or weekly, Pool Chemistry keeps you on track. Consistent monitoring is the foundation of trouble-free pool ownership.

The app comes pre-loaded with two weeks of sample readings and a full chemical inventory so you can explore every feature immediately. Configure your pool profile with volume (liters or gallons), pool type (in-ground, above-ground, or hot tub), and surface material for the most accurate dosage calculations.

All data is stored locally on your device for complete privacy and offline access — no account required, no cloud sync, no data collection. Pool Chemistry is built for pool owners who want professional-grade water management in a beautiful, easy-to-use package.
